🎸 Suggested Gig Performances

Professional Live Performance Setup Guide

Complete venue-specific performance configurations with equipment recommendations, setup strategies, and professional audio solutions for every gig scenario.

🏪 Small Venue

☕ Café / Coffee Shop

50–80 people. Acoustic-leaning set with 1–2 vocals, guitar, keys. Use minimal PA — 2 speakers, 2–4 mics. Bring your own mixer or use house PA.

🎛️ Recommended Setup:
  • 🎤 2× dynamic mics (vocals)
  • 🎸 1× DI for guitar/keys
  • 🎚️ Compact mixer (4–6 ch)
  • 🔊 Small Gig PA
View Small Venue Equipment ↗

🏪 Indoor

🍺 Pub / Bar

80–150 people. Full band: drums + guitar + bass + keys + vocals. Your PA setup (2× 10" active speakers) is ideal. Use 4–6 mics total.

🎛️ Recommended Setup:
  • 🎤 2× dynamic mics (vocals)
  • 🥁 1× kick mic, 1× overhead (drums)
  • 🎸 DI for bass and keys
  • 🎚️ Live Sound Setup
View Pub Equipment ↗

🎪 Private

🎉 House Party / Wedding

30–100 people. Versatile set: mix of covers and originals. Bring backing tracks via laptop/phone into mixer aux. LED panels add ambiance.

🎛️ Recommended Setup:
  • 🎤 2× dynamic mics
  • 📱 3.5mm aux for backing
  • 💡 LED panels for ambiance
  • 🎚️ Portable speaker stands
View Private Event Equipment ↗

🌳 Outdoor

🎊 Community Event

100–200 people. May need more power — consider renting 12" or 15" PA speakers. Same mixer and mics work; extend cable runs with 10m XLR.

🎛️ Recommended Setup:
  • 🔊 10m XLR for PA runs
  • ⚡ Surge-protected power
  • 🎛️ Monitor wedge (optional)
View Outdoor Event Equipment ↗
